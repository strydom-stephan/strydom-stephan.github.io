Sport

Cardio sports are my thing. I am a swimmer and almost swim more than I sleep. Eight sessions a week and three more dryland sessions on top of that keeps me quite busy and active. Music

I play three different instruments. Piano (my main instrument), Trombone (mainly for the orchestra) and guitar. I have been playing the piano for 9 years. I am currently busy with grade 7. Guitar has been a much slower journey. I stopped with guitar lessons a few months ago, so now I do improvisation on my own and learn a song here and there if I feel like it. Trombone I've only been doing for a year. My sight reading is quite good, making learning another instrument extremely easy. I also do grade 5 theory and sing in the choir.

Business

I started my own business at the end of lockdown. My only product is a 1l flask. Nurture's purpose is to provide the public with eco-friendly products and I can't wait to add a few other products to Nurture.

Creativity

This is definitely the least developed part in my life. I like to create. Whether it is composing a music piece or creating a website, creativity challenges my brain to think in different ways and allows me to express myself in ways that wouldn't be otherwise possible.
